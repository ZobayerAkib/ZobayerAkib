## 🜁 About Me

</div>

<img align="right" width="320" src="https://github-readme-stats.vercel.app/api?username=ZobayerAkib&show_icons=true&hide_title=true&hide_border=true&bg_color=0D0818&title_color=C4B5FD&text_color=A78BFA&icon_color=8B5CF6&rank_icon=github"/>

I'm **Md. Zobayer Ibna Kabir**, a Computer Science & Engineering graduate from **Ahsanullah University of Science and Technology**, working at the intersection of **Quality Assurance Engineering**, **Machine Learning**, and **Backend / Automation Engineering**.

I began my career as a **Junior Software Engineer, QA** at Universaltech Research & Development (a sister concern of London-based fintech **Mortgage Magic**), where I tested admin panels, iOS applications, and client portals — building a strong foundation in manual testing, API validation, and defect lifecycle management.

Alongside SQA, I actively research and build in **applied AI**, with an undergraduate thesis on gene–disease association prediction using graph embeddings, and three published works spanning explainable medical imaging and NLP.

**🔬 Research Interests**
- Computer Vision & Medical Imaging
- Multimodal Large Language Models (MLLMs)
- Explainable AI (XAI)
- AI for Social Good

**🧠 Core Expertise**
- Manual & API Software Quality Assurance
- Machine Learning / Deep Learning (Scikit-Learn, TensorFlow, Keras)
- NLP & LLM tooling (Hugging Face Transformers, LangChain, GPT APIs, CrewAI)
- Backend & Automation Engineering (FastAPI, Flask, n8n)
- Data Analysis & BI (Pandas, Power BI, Looker Studio)

**🎯 Product Engineering Mindset**
I approach every build like a QA engineer first — designing for testability, edge cases, and reliability before optimizing for features.

**📌 Open To**
`Software QA Engineer` · `ML / AI Engineer` · `Backend Engineer` · `Data Analyst` · `Research Collaborations`
